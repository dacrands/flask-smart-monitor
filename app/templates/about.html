What is ToViewIt?

It's functional art

The functionality comes from being able to view real-time weather information and the scrolling stock-ticker. The art comes from the configurable backrgound video.

It's customizable

Easily configure your location and stock-ticker.

How to use it

There are a number of ways run this application. Your configuration will largely depend on your technical knowledge, though even someone with no programming experience can use this applicaton.

Level I

Simply open in your favorite browser, hit (F11) to enter "full-screen" mode.

Level II

Connect a Raspberrypi a PC-monitor and run full-screen in the Chromium browser. This way you can use vcgencmd and cronjob to automate turning the designated monitor on/off according to your schedule.

Level III

A lot of improvements can be made to this application. Make a pull-request here — SmartFrame repo
